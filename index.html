About Me

Hi! My name is Mia. I'm a 16 year-old highschool student & aspiring developer living in Santa Rosa, California.

I spend most of my time studying, programming, and learning to program.

I have an app on the app store called Times Table Math. It won the Congressional App Challenge in 2016 and has been written about in the Press Democrat.

Since then, I have built this blog to learn Ruby on Rails, but I plan to use it to document my journey of programming and creating future projects.

You can contact me on Twitter at @johanssonmia9 or Instagram at @miajohansson9. Or feel free to email me at @mia@theteenmagazine.com
